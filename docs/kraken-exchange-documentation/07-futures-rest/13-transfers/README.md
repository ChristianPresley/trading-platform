# Transfers

Fund transfer and withdrawal endpoints -- move assets between accounts, subaccounts, and Spot wallets.

## Contents

1. [Sub Account Transfer](01_Sub-Account-Transfer.md) -- Transfer funds between master and subaccounts, or between margin and cash accounts.
   - `POST /transfer/subaccount`
2. [Transfer](02_Transfer.md) -- Transfer funds between margin accounts or between a margin and cash account.
   - `POST /transfer`
3. [Withdrawal](03_Withdrawal.md) -- Withdraw digital assets from the Futures platform to the Kraken Spot wallet.
   - `POST /withdrawal`
