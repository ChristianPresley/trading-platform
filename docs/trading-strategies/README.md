# Trading Strategies

Comprehensive catalog of trading strategies sourced from free, professional-grade resources. Each strategy is documented with trading rules, performance metrics, efficacy ratings, and cryptocurrency/24-7 market applicability.

**Total Strategies**: 120 | **Total Categories**: 19 | **Crypto-Applicable**: 73+

## Evidence Tiers

Strategies are separated into two tiers based on the quality and type of supporting evidence:

- **Academically Proven** — Backed by peer-reviewed papers, whitepapers, and/or independent backtests with academic citations
- **Practitioner-Tested** — Validated through practitioner backtesting without formal academic paper support

## Efficacy Rating System

| Rating | Meaning | Criteria |
|--------|---------|----------|
| 5/5 | Exceptional | Strong academic evidence, robust out-of-sample results, multiple confirming studies |
| 4/5 | Strong | Academic paper + solid backtest, minor OOS degradation |
| 3/5 | Moderate | Backtested with good results, limited academic support |
| 2/5 | Weak | Backtested but mixed results or short history |
| 1/5 | Poor | Theoretical or poorly performing in backtests |

## Crypto/24-7 Applicability

Each strategy is flagged with one of:

- **Yes** — Explicitly designed for or tested on cryptocurrency/24-7 markets
- **Adaptable** — Works in traditional markets, transferable to crypto with modifications
- **No** — Requires market structure not present in crypto (e.g., earnings, options expiration)
